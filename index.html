Key Assumptions
  • Selling price: 149,000 ₫ / pack (15 patches) — sold via school vending machines, Shopee & TikTok Shop
  • Variable cost: 7,000 ₫ / patch — 6,000 ₫ production + ~1,000 ₫ for platform fees, discounts & marketing campaigns
  • Fixed cost: 900,000 ₫ / month — 6 vending machine slots × 3 school machines
  • Salaries: 0 ₫ — profit-sharing model replaces fixed wages; higher contributors receive higher profit share
  • Business address: 0 ₫ — member's home address used as registered company address
  • Storage: 1,200 packs packed into 33 noodle boxes, distributed among 12 members (3 boxes each) at room temperature
  • Total annual cost: 146,545,000 ₫ + (900,000 ₫ × 12) = 157,345,000 ₫
  • First batch unit cost: 6,000 ₫ / patch (fixed-price factory deal — excluded from 30% contingency buffer)
